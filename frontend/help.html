Your Bookings


The Your Bookings page displays all past and current bookings that have been made on your account.


Each entry on this page is an individual booking that has been made. Displaying what type of class is booked, what date the class is scheduled for, what time the class will be held, and a button that can be used to cancel your booking.


Upon clicking the cancel booking button it will take you to a page that will display two additional buttons that will prompt you to confirm that you would like to cancel this class of if you would like to go back to the previous page.
